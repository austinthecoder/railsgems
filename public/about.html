What is this site?

A common scenario for me and a lot of people is finding the right tool for the job. In Ruby on Rails, that tool usually comes in the form of a RubyGem. There are thousands of RubyGems, but not all are useful for, or related to, the Rails framework. A vast majority are not. The purpose of this site is to compile and tag these gems to make it easy to find the right tool.

Built by Austin Schneider using Ruby on Rails.
